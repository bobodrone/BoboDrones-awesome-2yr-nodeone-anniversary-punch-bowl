Teaser
The magic moment when all the strawberrys turn blue is worth waiting for!

Ingredients:
8 bottles (0.75L) of dry champage or any really dry sparkling wine
1 bottle (0.70L) Cointreau (40%)
0-9.5 dl soda water (depending on wanted strength!)
5dl semi frozen strawberrys
5dl green wine graphes
16 sugar cubes
1 small bottle of blue caramel colour

Magic momento:
Cool down the sparkling wine to around 1 deg. celcius
Desolve the sugar cubes in the Cointreau
Cut up the semi frozen strawberrys
Divide the green wine grapes in halves
10-15 minutes before serving: put all ingredients together in a big bowl except the blue color
Try carefully drop by drop adding the blue color until you have the wanted shade
Add optional ice
Stir gently all night long, and serve your friends.
